Special Character

In html special charaters are used to represents characters that have a special meaning in html or that cant be easily expressed using the standard keybaord characters. This charaters are replaced with their correspnding html entities to ensure proper rendering in web browsers

Here are some commonly used special characters used in html:

  • '<'
  • >
  • &
  • "
  • '
  •  
  • ©
  • ®
  • ™
  • ¢
  • £
  • ¥
  • €
